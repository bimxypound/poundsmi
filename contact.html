Travel Information

  • If you’re planning an international trip, keep us informed by stopping by a member center or giving our Virtual Member Center a call at 800.442.2800 to let us know to expect transactions from your desired destination.
  • Beware of blocked and sanctioned countries. For a current list of these countries check out the Office of Foreign Asset Control (OFAC) of the US Department of Treasury’s list. If the country you are visiting is on this list, ALL card activity will be blocked per the requirement from OFAC. On occasion, Honor Credit Union may block countries in addition to those listed on the OFAC list, but should there be additional countries, they would be listed here.

Fraud Protection

  • If you suspect fraud you can turn your card off instantly from within the Honor mobile app.
  • Honor debit and credit card holders are automatically enrolled in our free card protection program. Cardholders can sign up for additional free MasterCard fraud services.
  • If we suspect fraudulent activity, the transaction will likely be declined and your account will be blocked to prevent further harmful activity. We will attempt to notify you as quickly as 30 seconds after a suspicious transaction takes place.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Honor's Routing Number?

272484852. This number is used for all Honor accounts.

How do I open an account?

Opening an account and switching to Honor has never been easier! Our simple application process takes just minutes to complete and our ClickSWITCH tool makes it as easy as clicking a button to switch your direct deposit, automatic bill payments, and other automated account services.

How do I reorder checks?

There are three ways to reorder checks!

Online Banking

  1. Login to Online Banking
  2. Click the orange “OC” icon next to Checking on the main Account Summary page.

Deluxe Website

You can reorder checks by visiting the Deluxe website.

In Person

You can stop by any local member center and speak with an Honor team member to order more checks.

How do I switch my direct deposit?

New Members

If you are joining Honor as a new member our ClickSWITCH tool will help you easily switch your direct deposit.

Existing Members

If you just got a new job with a new employer, switching your direct deposit is as simple as filling out our direct deposit form and handing it to your payroll department.

Direct Deposit Form

How do I check my credit score?

One of the many perks of being an Honor Credit Union member is we provide you with your updated VantageScore four times per year, completely free. These quarterly updates are done through soft pulls of your credit, so rest assured these have absolutely no impact on your score itself. Here are two easy steps to check your score.

Within Online Banking

  1. Login to Online Banking on your desktop computer
  2. Under the My Accounts menu tab select Credit Score

Within The Honor App

    1. Login
    2. Tap More, and then tap “View Credit Score”

Please Note: VantageScore is not available in mobile online banking in mobile browsers. Your credit history will not be provided with your credit score in Online Banking. You can view your full credit report for free once per year at AnnualCreditReport.com.

I lost my card. What do I do?

First, don’t panic! We’ve all been there. Give us a call at 800.442.2800 for assistance. If it’s after normal business hours please follow the prompts for after hours card support.

You also have the ability to turn your card on and off from within our mobile app.
